Privacy Policy

DCW Financial Inc ("DCW Financial," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use and safeguard it, and the choices you have. It applies to this website and the quote request services we provide.

We handle personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), applicable provincial privacy legislation, and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

1. Information We Collect

When you use our quote tool or contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact details you provide, such as your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Quote information you enter, such as the type and amount of coverage you're interested in, coverage length, tobacco use, your province, and your date of birth. This information is used to prepare an accurate quote.
  • Technical and usage data, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and how you interact with the site, collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Marketing source data, such as the ad or link that brought you here, so we understand which of our campaigns are working.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Prepare and provide the life insurance quote you requested.
  • Connect you with a licensed DCW Financial advisor who can review your options with you.
  • Contact you by phone, text message, or email about your request, as you consented to when you submitted the form.
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer service.
  • Improve our website, our services, and our marketing.
  • Meet our legal, regulatory, and insurance industry obligations.

3. Consent

By submitting your information through our quote form, you consent to being contacted by DCW Financial and its licensed advisors by phone, text, and email regarding your quote and related insurance products. You may withdraw your consent at any time (see Your Choices below). Withdrawing consent may limit our ability to provide you with a quote or service.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to deliver the service you asked for, including with:

  • Licensed advisors and agents affiliated with DCW Financial who will prepare your quote and follow up with you.
  • Insurance carriers whose products you ask us to quote, where necessary to obtain that quote or issue coverage.
  • Service providers that help us operate, such as customer relationship management, hosting, email, and analytics providers, who are bound to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.
  • Authorities or regulators, where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to make the site work, remember your progress, measure performance, and understand how our ads perform. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect how the site functions.

6. Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, to maintain our business records, and to meet legal and regulatory requirements. When it is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to securely delete or anonymize it.

7. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information against loss, theft, and unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to keep your information safe.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you and ask that it be corrected if it's inaccurate.
  • Withdraw your consent and ask us to stop contacting you, by replying STOP to a text, using the unsubscribe link in an email, or contacting us directly.
  • Ask us to delete your personal information, subject to our legal and regulatory obligations.

To make any of these requests, or if you have questions about how your information is handled, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
